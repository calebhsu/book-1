Make Food

Restaurant? Home cook? We'll match you with potential customers.

Launching in San Francisco in Spring 2016, customers can find Asian restaurants and cooks based on rating, location, menu, style, and open hours. Restaurants can pay for listing positions, while customers only pay the price for food and delivery directly to the restaurant.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • info_outlineWhat services do you provide?

    We recommend and match hungry customers to nearby Asian restaurants and cooks.

  • perm_identityWho can use the services?

    Hungry customers & businesses can order food through Hungry Asians.

  • group_workWho can join as service providers?

    Restaurants and home cooks can cook, deliver, and charge for meals.

  • credit_cardWhat prices do customers pay for service?

    Customers provide the upfront price for their order based on prices set by the restaurant.

  • storeHow much can a service provider earn?

    The amount earned by a restaurant or home cook is solely determined by the prices they set, and the number of orders they receive.
